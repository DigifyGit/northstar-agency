# NorthStar Hero V11 - Concept G Build Brief

## Concept Name
Verification Press

## Objective
Make verification/filtering physically understandable and authority-driven.

## Prompt (locked)
```text
4K, 16:9 ultra premium 2026 hero for “NorthStar”.
Hard reset: NO space, NO skyline, NO nebula, NO swirls, NO orbit arcs, NO constellations.
Background: deep navy #050914 studio gradient, clean.

Visual concept: VERIFICATION PRESS.
Composition: left 45 percent features a precision industrial press mechanism, right 55 percent clean negative space.

Center-left: the precision 16-point compass rose North Star sits on a circular die plate (machined titanium).
Above it: a thick transparent glass press block descends slightly, mid-action, implying “verification”.
Around the press: 5 small machined “module tokens” arranged in a tidy arc, each engraved:
MISSION CONTROL, SKILL RECOVERY LAB, CASE COMMAND, STRATEGIC ROADMAP, NEXT 7 DAYS
Two tokens show embossed stamps: VERIFIED and RISK (like quality control).

Material language: titanium, smoked glass, matte black ceramic, restrained indigo edge accent #5F6AD9 on the North tip only.
Lighting logic: crisp studio key from upper-left, sharper shadows than prior concepts, high authority, no glow effects.

Meaning layer: tiny debris-free micro-etching on the die plate reads “AUDITABLE” and “EVIDENCE”, extremely subtle, premium engraving, not typography art.
No UI, no charts, no grids.

Right side UI overlay standard.
Negative: no neon, no lens flare, no particles, no sci-fi environment.
```

## Variations
- A: heavier mechanical presence.
- B: cleaner minimal press geometry with more negative space.

## QA checklist
- Press motion meaning is obvious in under 3 seconds.
- Verification state must read as process quality, not decoration.
- No dashboard/chart fallback.
