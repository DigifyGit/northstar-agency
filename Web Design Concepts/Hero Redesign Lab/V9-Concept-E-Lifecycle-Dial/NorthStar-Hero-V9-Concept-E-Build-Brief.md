# NorthStar Hero V9 - Concept E Build Brief

## Concept Name
Lifecycle Engine Dial

## Objective
Show NorthStar as a structured lifecycle system with labeled operational modules and visible verification state.

## Prompt (locked)
```text
4K, 16:9 ultra premium 2026 hero for “NorthStar”, B2B Talent Intelligence Infrastructure.
Hard reset: NO space, NO skyline, NO nebula, NO starscape, NO grids, NO wireframes, NO abstract spirals.
Background: deep navy #050914 to near-black gradient, studio product photography look, subtle vignette, ultra clean.

Visual concept: LIFECYCLE ENGINE DIAL.
Composition: centered-left mechanical dial occupying 45 percent width, right 55 percent is clean negative space for typography and buttons.
At the center: a precision 16-point compass rose North Star (8 long points + 8 smaller points), aerospace geometry, razor symmetry.
Material language: brushed titanium + matte ceramic + smoked glass, minimal glow, micro-engraved details, premium machining.

Around the star: a large circular rotary dial with 5 thick segments (not a UI chart).
Each segment is a physical module plate with engraved label, highly legible:
MISSION CONTROL
SKILL RECOVERY LAB
CASE COMMAND
STRATEGIC ROADMAP
NEXT 7 DAYS
Each plate has a tiny abstract icon as an engraved mark only (no cartoons, no people, no avatars).
Show subtle “verification gate” stamps embossed on two plates only: VERIFIED and RISK, like quality-control marks, not neon.

Lighting logic: single soft key light from upper-left, hard micro-contrast, controlled specular highlights, no HDR bloom, no lens flare.
Depth: star sits slightly recessed, dial ring sits above it, a single slim indicator needle points to one module, implying progress through phases.

Right side UI overlay only (clean, expensive):
Navbar glass bar: NORTHSTAR | Platform | Methodology | Enterprise Solutions | Intelligence | Log In
Headline: Architecting the Future of Workforce (gradient only on “Future of Workforce” #7BC0D7 to #6E82CB)
Subtext: Precision Talent Intelligence. Verified Execution.
Buttons: primary subtle gradient #4A87AB to #294E74, secondary outline.

Negative constraints: no crypto, no web3, no gaming, no rainbow gradients, no clutter, no sci-fi city, no space dust, no floating particles.
```

## Variations
- A: strict enterprise polish, lower contrast metal.
- B: stronger tactile machining and deeper shadowing.

## QA checklist
- No chart-like appearance.
- Module labels immediately readable.
- Verification marks visible and subtle.
- Zero cosmic motifs.
