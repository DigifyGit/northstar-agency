# NorthStar Hero Loop Reset - Concept Family E to I Execution Plan

## Intent
Hard reset hero direction from decorative sci-fi style to a clear executive command-center product metaphor.

## Locked Constraints
- No nebula/space/cosmic wallpaper.
- No skyline, no constellation dots, no abstract swirls/trails.
- No generic dashboard-card repetition.
- Product modules must be legible and named.
- Verification gates must be visible and understandable.

## Mandatory Product Language
- Mission Control
- Skill Recovery Lab
- Case Command
- Strategic Roadmap
- Next 7 Days
- Verification gates:
  - VERIFIED_LEGIT
  - VERIFIED_RISK
  - UNKNOWN_INSUFFICIENT_EVIDENCE

## Visual System Baseline
- Background: deep navy #050914 to near-black.
- Style: studio product-photography lighting, physically understandable materials.
- Material palette: brushed titanium, matte ceramic, smoked/frosted glass.
- Accent restraint: indigo #5F6AD9 and teal #3FC8B4 as micro accents only.

## UI Overlay Baseline
- Navbar:
  - NORTHSTAR | Platform | Methodology | Enterprise Solutions | Intelligence | Log In
- Hero text:
  - Headline: Architecting the Future of Workforce
  - Gradient only on "Future of Workforce" (#7BC0D7 -> #6E82CB)
  - Subtext: Precision Talent Intelligence. Verified Execution.
- CTAs:
  - Primary: subtle gradient (#4A87AB -> #294E74)
  - Secondary: outline

## Variation Rule
For each concept, create 2 internal variations before selecting final:
- Variation A: conservative enterprise minimal
- Variation B: stronger material contrast and depth
Pick winner by readability + conversion clarity, not decoration.

## QA Gate (must pass)
- Module labels readable at normal desktop zoom.
- Verification state visible without explanation.
- Visual metaphor understandable in under 3 seconds.
- No overloaded text blocks.
- No fallback to old cosmic pattern.
