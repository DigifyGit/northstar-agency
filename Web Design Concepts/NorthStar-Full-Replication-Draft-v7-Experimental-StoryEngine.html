Section 6: Process Preview

A clear system from intake to offer

1. Intake

Define role targets, constraints, and success metrics.

Output: target profile and sourcing spec.

2. Sourcing

Build a curated pipeline of qualified prospects.

Output: ranked opportunities and candidate set.

3. Verification

Validate identity, evidence, and risk signals.

Output: verification status with reasoning.

4. Interview

Prepare and calibrate to reduce false positives.

Output: interview pack and scorecard framing.

5. Offer

Compare tradeoffs with evidence, not guesswork.

Output: decision support and next actions.
