FAQ

Questions, answered clearly

Is NorthStar a staffing agency?+
NorthStar is a talent intelligence infrastructure layer. It can support recruiting and staffing workflows, but the product is built around verification, transparency, and decision support — not commodity placement.
Do you work with technical roles?+
Yes. NorthStar is optimized for technical and high-trust hiring environments including engineering, security, data, and leadership roles.
How does verification work?+
Verification is evidence-first. We require a clear evidence table before any candidate progresses. If evidence is insufficient, the system marks that reality — no candidate advances on assumptions.
How do you handle fraud risk?+
Fraud signals are surfaced early and integrated into every pipeline gate. Identity anomalies are flagged with reasoning and logged in the audit trail, protecting your team from blind progression.
What does the reporting look like?+
Reporting is outcome-focused and role-based. Clients see next actions and pipeline status. Executives see conversion rates, risk exposure, and cycle performance. Everything is traceable.
