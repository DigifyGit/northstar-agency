Verification Policy

How NorthStar Prevents False Scam Labels and False Legitimacy

We verify before we classify. The objective is accuracy under uncertainty, not speed at the cost of wrong verdicts.

Hard Gate Requirements

Evidence Table

Source, capture date, observation, confidence level, and conflict notes.

Primary Source Check

Official company page, authoritative profile, or direct hard artifact review.

Artifact Rule

No scam label without a concrete artifact proving harmful or deceptive pattern.

Allowed Verdicts

VERIFIED_LEGIT

Cross-source consistency and no high-risk conflict found.

VERIFIED_RISK

Clear risk signals validated by artifacts and source triangulation.

UNKNOWN_INSUFFICIENT_EVIDENCE

Insufficient data to decide. Escalate and continue investigation.

Operating Rule

No evidence table, no verdict. No primary source check, no analysis. No hard artifact, no scam label.
