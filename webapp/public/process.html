Agency Roadmap

Start-to-End Workflow for Job Sourcing Execution

This timeline is built so a new team member or buyer can understand exactly how cases move from intake to accepted offer.

10-Step Delivery Sequence

Step 1

Intake and Positioning

Candidate profile, constraints, salary floor, location preferences, and urgency definition.

Step 2

Market and Role Mapping

Build role clusters and priority sectors by likelihood and compensation target.

Step 3

Sourcing and Lead Intake

Capture opportunities from boards, agency channels, LinkedIn, and direct company pages.

Step 4

Company Verification Gate

Identity checks, profile checks, website checks, and recruiter pattern checks with artifacts.

Step 5

Fit Scoring and Shortlisting

Score each role by alignment, risk, compensation fit, and progression probability.

Step 6

Application Package Execution

Deliver tailored CV bullets, outreach scripts, and submission instructions.

Step 7

Interview Enablement

Prep documents, question banks, scenario drills, and positioning narrative.

Step 8

Follow-up and Pipeline Control

Track application status and run structured follow-up cadence.

Step 9

Offer Decision Brief

Compensation and risk evaluation with recommended negotiation points.

Step 10

Outcome Learning Loop

Capture what worked and update strategy for next cycle speed.

Mandatory Gate Logic

No evidence table, no verdict. No primary source check, no analysis. No hard artifact, no scam label.
