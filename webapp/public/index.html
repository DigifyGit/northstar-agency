Fraud & Scam Protection Policy

Inspired by global recruitment scam warnings from top firms: we hard-gate risk decisions and do not allow assumption-based labels.

  • We never classify a company as scam without source-backed evidence.
  • No evidence table -> no verdict. No primary source check -> no analysis.
  • Risk alerts are documented with links, artifacts, and confidence labels.
  • Candidates receive verification status before application actions.
