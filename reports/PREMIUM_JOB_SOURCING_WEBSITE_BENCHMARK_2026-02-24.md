# Premium Job Sourcing Website Benchmark (10-Agency Baseline)

Date: 2026-02-24  
Scope: UI/content benchmark used to redesign NorthStar public website and sitemap.

## Objective
Build NorthStar's public-facing website from real premium staffing/executive-search patterns, not assumptions.

## Repeated Patterns Found Across Top Firms
- Clear split between job-seeker journey and employer/organization services.
- Strong hero section with value statement + direct CTA (search jobs, contact, speak to advisor).
- Multi-page information architecture (About, Services, Sectors/Industries, Insights, Contact).
- Trust layer via global scale signals, expertise claims, and operating credibility.
- Advisory content layer (insights, hiring trends, salary/career intelligence).
- Region/sector-based navigation to reduce user confusion.

## NorthStar Mapping Implemented
- Public sitemap: `index`, `about`, `services`, `process`, `industries`, `insights`, `fraud-protection`, `contact`.
- Persistent portal entry point: `Client Login` button in global nav.
- Candidate-first messaging with premium agency framing and operational clarity.
- Explicit anti-fraud and verification policy page to prevent assumption-driven verdicts.
- Process page that explains start-to-end delivery for new staff, buyers, and clients.

## Design Direction Implemented
- Enterprise dark blue/blue-gray theme with liquid-glass surfaces.
- Professional typography hierarchy, concise copy, and conversion-focused buttons.
- Responsive layout strategy for desktop, tablet, and smartphone.

## Constraint Notes
- This benchmark was used for structure and positioning patterns, not for copying proprietary text or branding.
