# L1 Behavioral Assessment

> Pattern basis: Adecco / LHH behavioral screens
> Expected response format: STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
> Score range: 0-3 per question (max 24)

## Scoring Rubric (0-3)
- 0: no concrete example, generic statements only.
- 1: example exists but weak structure and no measurable result.
- 2: clear STAR structure with relevant actions.
- 3: strong STAR structure, clear impact, and customer-centered judgment.

## Question 1 - Patience and empathy
- Question: Describe a time you handled a frustrated user while resolving a technical issue.
- STAR focus: de-escalation approach, language choice, final outcome.

## Question 2 - Problem-solving under uncertainty
- Question: Tell me about a time you had incomplete information but still solved a problem.
- STAR focus: assumptions, diagnostics, validation.

## Question 3 - Prioritization under pressure
- Question: Give an example of how you prioritized multiple urgent tickets.
- STAR focus: triage logic, communication, SLA awareness.

## Question 4 - Accountability
- Question: Describe a mistake you made in support and how you corrected it.
- STAR focus: ownership, corrective action, prevention.

## Question 5 - Communication clarity
- Question: Explain how you communicate technical issues to non-technical users.
- STAR focus: simplification, expectation-setting, confirmation.

## Question 6 - Adaptability
- Question: Share a time you adapted quickly to a new tool or process.
- STAR focus: learning speed, transfer to operations.

## Question 7 - Reliability and follow-through
- Question: Describe how you ensure follow-through after promising a callback.
- STAR focus: tracking discipline, completion proof.

## Question 8 - Continuous improvement mindset
- Question: Tell me about a time you improved a repetitive support workflow.
- STAR focus: bottleneck identification, change applied, impact.
