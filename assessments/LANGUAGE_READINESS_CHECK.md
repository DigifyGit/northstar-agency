# Language Readiness Check

## 1 · English Interview Simulation (3 scenarios)
1. Explain how you would troubleshoot a VPN issue for a remote employee.
2. Explain a ticket escalation you handled and why you escalated.
3. Describe how you would calm a frustrated end user while fixing a password issue.

## 2 · Portuguese Basic Conversation Check
1. Apresenta-te e descreve a tua experiencia em suporte tecnico.
2. Explica como resolves um problema de acesso ao e-mail.
3. Como comunicas com um utilizador frustrado?
